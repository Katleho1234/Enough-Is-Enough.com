We Are Here To Provide Voice To The Voiceless

Bullying is the repeated mistreatment of an individual by another or a group. It can take many forms, including physical, verbal, or emotional abuse. It can leave scars that last a lifetime.

Types of Bullying

Verbal bullying: hurtful words, teasing, name-calling, etc.

Physical bullying: hitting, punching, pushing, etc.

Social bullying: spreading rumours,mimicking unkindly,social exclude someone, etc.

Cyber bulling: the use of eletronic communication to bully a person, typically by sending of an intimidating or threatening nature.

Effects of Bullying

Bullying can have severe and long-lasting effects. These can include:

  • Suicidal-thoughts
  • Depression
  • Anxiety
